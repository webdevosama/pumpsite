Founded on Purpose

A Visionary Foundation by
Ibrahim Abu Hassanein

Founded in 1966, our journey began with a simple but powerful goal: to provide reliable water solutions to the heart of Sudan. Ibrahim Abu Hassanein recognized that the future of Sudanese agriculture and industry depended on precision engineering.

Today, we continue that legacy by partnering with world‑class manufacturers like Caprari and Sigma, ensuring that every pump we install is built for the harshest conditions.
